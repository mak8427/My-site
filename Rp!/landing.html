My Second Life as a Kingmaker

Embark on a high fantasy adventure where you reincarnate as a noble in a medieval fantasy world, starting from a remote village to face a demon king invasion. This Savage World / Vassal system game explores nation building, politics, technology development, war, society, and economy.

The Daughter of the Dragon

In the Drakoria Empire, humans are a resilient and versatile race, often subjugated under the dragon-descended Drakarys elite. Explore their rich history, complex society, and struggles in a magic-dominated world through the RP! system. Their survival and resilience are tested daily in this culturally rich setting.
