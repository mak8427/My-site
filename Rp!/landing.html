My Second Life as a Kingmaker

Embark on a high fantasy adventure where you reincarnate as a noble in a medieval fantasy world, starting from a remote village to face a demon king invasion. This Savage World / Vassal system game explores nation building, politics, technology development, war, society, and economy.

The Daughter of the Dragon

Welcome to the World of Alanré, rich of marvels, misteries and legends. Explore the varieties of races and cultures: legendary dragons, pious humans, monstroues giants, powerful dracarys and wise faries. Visit breath-taking landscapes where nature is threatened by humanity and it's trying to re-establish the balance. After the Ruin of Narveen, the eyes are towards the Dragon Emperor, meanwhile the survivors struggle to search a new home..
